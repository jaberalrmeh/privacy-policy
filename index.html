How we handle your data

We never sell your personal data to any third party, under any circumstance.

Your pregnancy data stays on your device by default — stored securely and privately.

If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted and stored securely on Firebase.

We do not share your health data with advertisers or marketing networks — ever.

You can delete all your data at any time directly from the Settings screen in the app.

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how to improve the app — this never includes your personal or health details.
