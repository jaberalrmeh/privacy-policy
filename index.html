Privacy Policy

Pregnancy Care Baby Tracker · Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pregnancy Care Baby Tracker ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the App. We built this App to help you track your pregnancy — your privacy matters to us, and we've designed the App to keep your personal health information under your control.

In short: Your pregnancy details, weight logs, symptoms, diary entries, and personal notes are stored only on your device. We do not upload, sell, or share this information with anyone.

We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use the core features of the App.

2. Push Notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), a service provided by Google, to send you optional reminders and notifications (for example, medicine or checkup reminders). To enable this, your device is assigned an anonymous device token and subscribed to notification topics. This token does not include your name, health data, or any personal information you enter in the App.

Firebase Cloud Messaging is governed by Google's own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

You can disable notifications at any time from your device's system settings.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store your health data (weight, symptoms, diary entries, pregnancy info) on any server.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your health information for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal and health-related information you enter is stored locally in the App's private storage on your device, using standard on-device storage mechanisms provided by the Android operating system. This data is not accessible to us, and we cannot view, recover, or transfer it.

If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

5. Data Export

The App allows you to export your data (for example, as a JSON file) so you can back it up or share it with your healthcare provider. This export is created and saved directly on your device or shared through apps you choose (e.g., email, messaging); we do not receive a copy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults tracking their own pregnancy and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may process limited technical data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — push notifications

8. Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • You can delete all your data by uninstalling the App.
  • You can use the App without providing any personal information beyond what you choose to enter for tracking purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
